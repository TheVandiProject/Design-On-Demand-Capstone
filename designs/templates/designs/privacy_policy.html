Privacy Policy

Information Collection

At Design On Demand, we may collect the following types of information from users:

  • Personal Information: When users interact with certain features of our website, such as placing an order or contacting customer support,
      we may collect personal information such as names, email addresses, and shipping addresses.
  • Non-Personal Information: We may also collect non-personal information such as IP addresses, browser types, and device information to
      improve our website's functionality and user experience.

    • Use of Information

    The information we collect may be used for the following purposes:

  • Personal Information: We use personal information to process orders, communicate with users, and provide customer support. We may also use this
      information to send promotional materials with the user's explicit consent.
  • Non-Personal Information: Non-personal information is used for analytics, tracking website performance, and making improvements to our services.

    • Data Security

    We take reasonable measures to protect the information collected from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures may
    include encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits.

    Cookies and Tracking

    We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance user experience. Users can manage their cookie preferences through their
    web browser settings.

    Third-Party Disclosure

    We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer users' information to third parties without their consent, except for trusted third parties who assist us
    in operating our website or conducting our business, as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

    User Rights

    Users have the right to access, correct, or delete their personal information. They may also opt out of receiving promotional communications from us.

    Changes to the Privacy Policy

    We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Changes and clarifications will take effect immediately upon their posting on the website.
